Abserny Mobile

The full power of offline object detection and natural voice feedback, now in your pocket. Available for Android — iOS coming soon.

What's in This Release

Everything from the desktop version, rebuilt for mobile with performance and accessibility at its core.

⚡

Optimized Inference

Mobile-tuned YOLOv8 nano runs entirely on-device — no internet, no cloud, no delay.

🎙️

Voice Activation

Hands-free operation with offline Vosk speech recognition. Say "start" and Abserny responds.

📳

Haptic Feedback

Vibration patterns complement voice output for richer, more accessible environmental awareness.

🔋

Battery Efficient

Smart listening mode pauses processing when idle, preserving battery for all-day use.

🔒

Fully Private

All processing is local. No data leaves your device — ever. No accounts, no tracking.

🗣️

Natural Language Output

Hear clear, conversational descriptions like "I see a person on your left and a chair ahead."

System Requirements

🤖 Android

Android 8.0 (Oreo) or higher
2 GB RAM minimum, 4 GB recommended
~300 MB storage for app + model

🍎 iOS

iOS 15 or higher
iPhone 8 or newer recommended
Coming soon — follow GitHub for updates

📷 Camera

Rear camera required for object detection
Works best with 12 MP or higher

🎤 Microphone

Required for voice activation
Works with built-in and external mics
